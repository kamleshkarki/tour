Cancellation & Refund Policy

We believe in complete fairness. Our policy is designed to protect both you and our team who pre-book services on your behalf. Please read carefully before booking.

1
Cancellation 30+ Days Before Travel ‚Äî Full Refund
If you cancel your booking 30 or more days before the scheduled travel date, you will receive a full refund of your advance payment minus a 5% processing/transaction fee. No questions asked.
2
Cancellation 15‚Äì29 Days Before Travel ‚Äî 50% Refund
If you cancel between 15 and 29 days before travel, you will receive a 50% refund of the advance paid. The remaining 50% covers hotel deposits and pre-booked vehicle costs that cannot be recovered.
3
Cancellation Under 15 Days ‚Äî No Refund
Cancellations within 14 days of travel are non-refundable as hotels, permits, and transport are fully committed by this point. We strongly recommend travel insurance for unforeseen circumstances.

If road blockages or severe weather during your trip cause a missed destination, we will adjust your itinerary at no extra cost and refund proportionally for any nights not delivered. Your safety is always priority.
